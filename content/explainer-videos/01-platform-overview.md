## Opening (0:00–0:20)

- Welcome to the OSU Finance Investment Club Analyst Platform.
- This is a learning-first equity research tool built for club members at Oregon State.
- Whether you're a first-time analyst or a returning member, this platform helps you build real research skills — not just run a model.

## Two Modes (0:20–0:50)

- **Teach Mode** — Step-by-step guidance. Every concept explained. Ideal for new analysts learning the process for the first time.
- **Analyst Mode** — Full access to all tools. Same best-practice structure, but you move at your own pace. "Why?" links and explainer videos are always available.
- You can switch modes at any time.

## The Workflow (0:50–1:40)

Walk through the four-step flow:

1. **Pick a company** — Enter a ticker. The platform validates data availability via SEC EDGAR.
2. **Articulate your thesis** — Before touching the model, define your catalyst, key assumptions, and bull/base/bear view. This drives everything.
3. **Build the model** — 3-statement model (IS/BS/CF) from SEC filings, then a DCF. For every key driver, you see Your Assumption vs Management vs Consensus.
4. **Compare to consensus** — See how your model outputs (revenue, EPS, target price) compare to Street consensus. Identify divergences and articulate why.

## Navigation (1:40–2:10)
